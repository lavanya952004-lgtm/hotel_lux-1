About HotelLux

HotelLux is a premier luxury hotel offering exceptional accommodations and world-class service. Established in 2010, our mission is to create unforgettable experiences for our guests with the highest level of comfort and hospitality.

Located in the heart of the city, HotelLux features elegant rooms, top-tier amenities, and dedicated staff who are always ready to assist. Whether you're here for business or leisure, we strive to make your stay truly memorable.
